SIDE PROJECTS

In my free time I try to upskill myself. These are some of the projects I'll be working on during summer.

DANISHCRIPT TO-DO-LIST FOR PARENTS WORLD CARDS JIMMY SAKURAI

With Danishcript, I've been trying to mix JavaScript and my Danish lessons.

With the To-Do-List For Parents, I've been practicing loops within loops. Basically you write something you would like to accomplish that day and boom, your child surprises you with something else.

With World Cards, I'm building the first marketplace to buy unique moments from different World Cups to own them as digital assets.

Finally, my last project spins around the story of Akio Sakurai, the greatest and most thorough imitator of Jimmy Page, the legendary guitar player from Led Zeppelin. I'm building a website that tells his story inside of a guitar you can even play by clicking the strings.
